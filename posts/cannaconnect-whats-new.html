Safety and crime prevention

  • Protecting your business from crime
    No business is immune from crime, but there are steps you can take to prevent it. Read our quick tips on protecting your business from crime and download a copy of our brochure to share with your employees and colleagues.
  • If you do experience a theft or break-in, make sure to notify local authorities. You also must report the event to the Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) within 24 hours of the incident with form DCC-LIC-028 as required by section 15036 of the DCC regulations.
