Type: Regular rulemaking  

Status:  Accepting public comments through July 11, 2023. 

The Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) is providing notice to the public of its intent to permanently adopt its emergency regulations that implements Assembly Bill 195 which requires that information relating to cannabis goods leaving a licensed retail premises in a delivery vehicle be recorded in the statewide track and trace system.  

View the rulemaking documents:  

  • Initial notice and proposal – May 26, 2023 
  • Notice of Proposed Rulemaking – Track and Trace Requirements for Delivery of Cannabis Goods (May 2023) 
  • Regulation Text – Track and Trace Requirements for Delivery of Cannabis Goods (May 2023) 
  • Initial Statement of Reasons – Track and Trace Requirements for Delivery of Cannabis Goods (May 2023) 

How to submit a public comment 

You can comment on the proposed regulations through Tuesday, July 11, 2023. You can submit comments by mail or email. You only need to submit your comment by one method.  

Send your comment to DCC, and reference the rulemaking topic (“track and trace delivery requirements”):  

  • By mail: DCC Legal Affairs Division, 2920 Kilgore Road, Rancho Cordova, CA 95670 
  • By email: publiccomment@cannabis.ca.gov 

You can also submit your comment verbally through a virtual public comment hearing which will be held:  

  • Tuesday, July 11, 2023, 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM 

Read tips for submitting a public comment. We’ve created a public comment template to make it easy for you to submit your feedback.
